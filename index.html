Empowering Communities Through Technology

The Just Us League Limited is a charity dedicated to providing access to robotics and AI education for underserved communities.

Our Mission

Fostering innovation and cultural preservation through technology.

The Just Us League Limited (ABN: 50603437467) is a registered charity committed to bridging the technology gap. We believe in learning by doing.

We donate access to state-of-the-art robotics kits and provide single-use kits to Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples, as well as other participants in our community meetup groups. Our goal is to inspire the next generation of innovators and thinkers.

Our programs are designed to be hands-on, engaging, and culturally relevant, ensuring that everyone has the opportunity to explore the exciting world of technology.

Key Projects

Innovating at the intersection of technology and culture.

The Dreamtime LLM Initiative

In collaboration with the Australian AI Society, we are pioneering the development of a Large Language Model (LLM) trained on Indigenous cultural histories.

The Dreamtime LLM project aims to preserve and revitalize ancient stories and knowledge systems by leveraging cutting-edge AI. It's a testament to how technology can be a powerful tool for cultural preservation and understanding.

Equipment Sharing Program

We are open to sharing our robotics equipment with schools and other community groups to broaden the reach of STEM education.

Requirements for Sharing

To ensure the safety of participants and the care of our equipment, we require appropriate adult supervision and valid insurance coverage from borrowing organizations. For more details email contact@jul.org.au
